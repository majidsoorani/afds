# AFDS Commercial License Terms (Summary)

This document is a commercial licensing summary for AFDS.
It is not legal advice and should be reviewed by legal counsel before use in contracts.

## 1. Purpose

AFDS is available under AGPL-3.0-only or a paid commercial license.
Organizations that do not want to comply with AGPL obligations must obtain a commercial license.

## 2. Trigger for Commercial License

A commercial license is required for one or more of the following:

- Production use in a business environment.
- Embedding AFDS in a proprietary product or service.
- Offering AFDS as part of a managed service.
- Any use where AGPL obligations are not acceptable.

## 3. Commercial Rights

Subject to contract execution and fees, the commercial license grants:

- Right to use AFDS in production.
- Right to keep modifications private.
- Right to distribute AFDS as part of licensed offerings.
- Access to enterprise support/SLA if purchased.

## 4. Exclusions

Unless explicitly included in a signed order form:

- No trademark rights.
- No indemnity.
- No guaranteed support response times.
- No rights to third-party proprietary data feeds.

## 5. Third-Party Data and Services

Connectors may require separate vendor agreements and API keys.
You are responsible for complying with third-party terms.

## 6. Contact

For commercial licensing, support plans, and deployment options,
contact the AFDS maintainers.
