# Code of Conduct

This project follows a respectful, inclusive collaboration standard.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Accept and act on feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive or abusive communication.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors for violations.
